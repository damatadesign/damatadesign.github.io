Les Mignons Pompons

Les Mignons Pompons is a non-profit organization that rescues abandoned and neglected rabbits. I had the opportunity to work with them in order to improve their brand identity by creating a logo and design their merchandise. The organization wanted a logo that reflected their charming rescue and that's dedicated to the first rabbit they rescued, Wendy.

The softwares used were Illustrator and Photoshop.

The challenges for this project was to maintain the detailed look of a rabbit that the client requested, while creating a memorable and versatile logo.
