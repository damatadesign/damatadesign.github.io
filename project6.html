Milk Campaign

The mandate for this project was to create a promotional poster for a milk campaign that presented the product as a natural source of comfort for people. I wanted to represent the comfort of milk as a warm cozy blanket. Mimicking its checkered look and polygonal shapes, I created a pattern in the style of a quilted blanket with milk-related illustrations. Furthermore, I was also inspired by the milky way and decided to make my promotional poster cosmos-themed, with the slogan "Taste the heavens".

The softwares used were Illustrator and Photoshop.

The challenges for this project were creating the imagery of the magazine. Since many of the sightings date back decades ago, the photographs were limited and so I had to work with a small selection of images.
