Café chez toi

The purpose of this project was to create the logo and branding of a coffee shop. I named my company "Café chez toi" to bring a sense of comfort to the customer, that only their own home can offer. The botanical elements in the logo represent the organic and eco-friendly values that "Café chez toi" holds.

The softwares used were Illustrator and Indesign.

The challenges for this project were creating the imagery of the magazine. Since many of the sightings date back decades ago, the photographs were limited and so I had to work with a small selection of images.
