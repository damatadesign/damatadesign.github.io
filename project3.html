Life's Postcards

Using my family's vintage photos, Life's Postcards is a personal project where I created distinct and personal collages as postcards. Postcards are a method of communication in order for people to commemorate a physical place they've traveled to. Using that same concept, I wanted to cherish the important moments in a person's lifetime as different places in time.

The softwares used were Illustrator and Photoshop. However, a lot of the work was done using paper objects around my house and combining them to make an interesting collage.

The biggest challenge was to find the correct imagery for the concept of my project.
