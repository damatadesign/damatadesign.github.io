Les Savons Célestes

The mandate for this project was to create a logo for our soap company and design the packaging for three different fragrances. Savons Célestes is a soap company that focuses on empowering women and encouraging them to unleash their inner goddess. Using only medicinal plants, each soap is uniquely based on a different goddess of mythology, with their symbolic plant used as the main ingredient. For the first product launch, the theme is the goddesses of greek mythology: Aphrodite, Athena and Artemis.

The softwares used were Illustrator and Indesign.
