"Godspeed You Black Emperor" Poster and Video

This was a personal project where I designed a poster inspired by the Montreal-based music collective called "Godspeed You Black Emperor!".Using some of their original illustrations, I designed a poster using photos I took of churches from all over Montreal. Also, I created a video centred around their song "Storm" using the visuals of the poster.

The softwares used were Illustrator and Photoshop.

The challenges for this project were creating the imagery of the magazine. Since many of the sightings date back decades ago, the photographs were limited and so I had to work with a small selection of images.
