Graduation Campaign

The mandate for this project was to create a graduation campaign for Lasalle's graphic design program. The concept behind my tryptic posters was to represent our jump into the vast world of design. Since our whole school year has been done from home, graduation will bring us a true sense of freedom. Part of the campaign was to create an animated video using our work from our posters. I decided to combine the illustrations of all my posters to create my promotional video for Lasalle College.

The softwares used were Illustrator and Aftereffects.

The challenge for this project was the animation video. It was especially hard for me to time the positioning with the music. .
